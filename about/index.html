About my Blog

This blog serves as a repository for all my personal projects. It is designed to be informal and irregularly updated. As a result, I will use informal language and be sparse on proofreading. If I err in the grammatical sense, please do not hesitate to warn me in the comments.

My blog will never serve ads. However, its comments are powered by Disqus, which some extensions, especially Privacy Badger, will block for tracking. If you want to write a comment and cannot find the comment section, please try disabling your advertisement and tracking blockers first.

About Me

Hi, I’m Dillon Morse, a junior at the Academy for Science and Design, where I study computer science, chemistry, and calculus. If you met me online, you probably know me as Ignotis, an active botmaker on the Awesomenauts forums. I have a strong interest in artificial intelligence and, more broadly, computer systems and programming. While I spend most of my free time on personal projects, I also enjoy casual gaming and action RPGs.
